You might expect that the $100,000 watches from Trump would be delivered from Florida, where the former U.S. President resides, as businesses are generally managed from the owner's location. However, the Trump watches are managed through a Wyoming LLC, a type of company many people choose to keep their assets out of the spotlight.

Limited Liability Companies or LLCs are incredibly popular. Look at how popular they are:

For years, Wyoming has flown under the radar while Delaware got much attention for its corporate friendly legislation and its high number of LLC. However, Wyoming has been catching up to the point of surpassing Delaware in number of new incorporations, including LLCs, in 2023.

While it may be surprising for some, this change did not happened overnight. Have a look at the number of new incoporations per capita in Wyoming compared to the US average (not counting Delaware, Wyoming and Puerto Rico).
